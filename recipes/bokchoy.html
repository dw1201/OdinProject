Stir Fried Bok Choy

Recipe for Stir Fried Bok Choy

Bok choy is one of the many kinds of chinese cabbage and is highly resistant to cold weather. The name literally translates to white (bok) vegetable (choy).

Ingredients for a serving size of 4

  • 1lb baby bok choy (larger bok choy works too)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2tbsp corn oil
  • 0.50tsp salt
  • 0.50tsp sugar
  • 0.50tsp chicken bouillon(optional)

    • Note: baby bok choy is my preference but the larger varieties also work.

Directions

  1. Wash bok choy

    • Peel away the individual leafs and thoroughly wash as dirt gets trapped along the root.
  2. Dry and cut the bok choy in half length wise.
  3. Heat oil in a woke or pan and mince garlic.
  4. Cook garlic once oil starts to shimmer and add bok choy in a few seconds later.
  5. Add seasoning then plate. This is especially important with salt. Salt will cause the bok choy to immediately release moisture.
