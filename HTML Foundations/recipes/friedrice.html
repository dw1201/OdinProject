Ginger Fried Rice

Recipe for ginger fried rice.

Ingredients for a serving size of 4

    Main Ingredients
  • 2oz ginger
  • 3oz chicken
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt (for eggs)
  • 2 cups white rice (uncooked)
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt (for cooking)

Chicken Marinade
  • 1tbsp water
  • 1tsp cornstarch
  • 0.50tsp salt
    •

    Directions

    1. Cook rice
    2. Cut chicken
    3. Marinate chicken
    4. Mince ginger
    5. Cut green onion
    6. Prepare eggs
    7. Start cooking eggs
    8. Cook chicken
    9. Add ginger to wok
    10. Add rice to wok
    11. Add salt and eggs
    12. Add green onion
    13. Plate dish
